Who we are About ES Special

ES Special focuses on highly targeted Chrome extensions for video-centric workflows, built and maintained by jeffestw.

ES Special is a small extension studio created by jeffestw, focused on tools for people who pause, zoom and analyze video frames every day ‚Äì from developers and designers to gamers and content creators.

The goal is simple: combine a clean, minimal UI with controls that feel like a dedicated desktop app, but available directly in your browser as a Chrome extension.

What we build Zoom + enhancer tools for video platforms, smart extension managers and experimental players that give you more control without cluttering your browser.
